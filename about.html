My Story

My name is Michael Emmons and I grew up and lived in upstate New York for 24 years. Shortly after obtaining a Bachelor's degree in Communications from SUNY Cortland, I moved from my northern roots and headed south to Charlotte, North Carolina. The move was motivated by a lackluster job market followed by a devastating recession.

After a few months moving down to Charlotte, I was able to obtain a job in the finance industry through Wells Fargo. From there I have built relationships with my clients and developed professional strengths. Being in the finance industry as a banker and a Home Equity lending specialist for 5 years has allowed me to gain valuable experience. I was able to work with an affluent clientele due to my background in financial consulting, leadership, management, sales, and customer service. Though I had a stellar reputation with my managers and peers, I still found myself lacking passion in my current profession. To fill this void, I began looking at different careers. I knew I wanted something that was challenging yet also creative.

That new passion is web development. I recently completed web development program through UNCC with the purpose of becoming a full stack developer. This new purpose and passion has allowed me to receive the challenge of non-stop learning and use of my creative side.

As you go through my site you will see projects I have worked on that incorporate HTML JAVASCRIPT, CSS, NODE JS, FIREBASE, MYSQL, EXPRESS JS, SEQUELIZE, MONGO DB and REACT JS. There are links located to the right of the page to view my Linkedin, Github, and Stackoverflow. If you have any questions or comments please visit my Contact page and send me a message!
